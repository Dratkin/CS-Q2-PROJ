Grade 7. SY 2020-2021

It would probably take an entire essay to describe my experience as a grade 7 scholar but to sum it up nicely, it was a school year filled with new experiences. I got to experience a little bit of what it was like in PISAY. I also had to adjust and conform to an entirely new set of demands and expectations that didn't just come with being a PISAY scholar, but being in high school as a whole. I had to get used to constantly having something to do and rarely having any time for myself. Because of this, I describe this school year as a year of adjustment and adaption to the new environment I had found myself in.

Grade 8. SY 2021-2022

This school year was a good year for me academic-wise since early on in the year was when I had fully adapted to life in PISAY. This allowed me to achieve what I consider to be the perfect balance between my academic life and my personal life. I was able to focus on my academic tasks as a scholar as well as the preservation of my mental health. This lead me to make and achieve great things during the school year, not least my achievement of the "Top DL" title and although it didn't really affect me that much, it gave me a huge motivation boost. Overall, I can only describe the year as a good and healthy year for me.

Grade 9. SY 2022-2023

Now, I know that we still aren't finished with the 9th grade yet and I know that we still have 2 more quarters to experience however, I just want to share my experience with this school year so far especially considering that this is our first face-to-face school year here in PISAY. First of all, our entire batch is finally getting a chance to socialize with each other and I'm finally getting the chance to properly catch up with my friends after 2 years in quarantine. Our batch is also finally experiencing what it's like to have face-to-face class discussions which are, in my opinion, better than online class discussions. I have also noticed that the workload that the teachers put on us students is much more bearable than the workload during online classes. Overall, it's been a good experience so far and I only wonder what's coming up next.
